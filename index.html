About Me

Finance, Entrepreneurship,
and Everything In Between

I'm a senior at the University of Miami Herbert Business School, graduating with a BBA in Entrepreneurship and a Minor in Real Estate. In June, I'm joining Point72 Asset Management as a Private Credit Analyst in New York — a role I've been building toward through three years of consecutive internships across real estate debt, private equity, and institutional credit.

My career started at Silverstein Properties, where I was the youngest person on the debt team by a significant margin. I didn't have the background the people around me had, so I went and built it — on my own time, through reading, asking questions, and doing the work. That experience set the tone for everything that followed.

Outside of finance, I'm involved in my family's payment processing business, where I work on business development and client acquisition. I'm also a part-time DJ — I've played venues like LIV in Miami — and I'm an active investor, closely following names like CoreWeave and Eli Lilly as AI and healthcare converge with the market volatility I find genuinely fascinating.
